SacTraffic Widget

Want a widget of SacTraffic.org incidents for your site? One like this:

Simple, just add the following to your site, right where you want it:

<script src="http://www.sactraffic.org/javascript/sactraffic-widget.min.js"></script>

Is it ugly? So customize it

We have themes:

  • Default - the cool blue default theme of SacTraffic.org.
  • Lectroid - Black and Orange like my favorite site.
  • None - You don't need no stinking theme, you got the CSS covered.
